FAQ

Common questions

Are the classes live or recorded?

Both. Every session is taught live on Zoom so you can ask questions in real time. Recordings are uploaded the same day so you can rewatch as many times as you want right up to the exam.

What if I can't attend a class live?

No problem. Every session is recorded and uploaded the same day. You get full access to the recordings to catch up in your own time.

How long is each session?

Each session is around 6 hours long. We work until everything is covered. These are not 50-minute school periods. They are full intensive sessions designed to leave nothing on the table.

Are there really limited seats?

Yes. Last year we had hundreds of students in every class. This year classes are heavily moderated and capped so they run smoothly and every student gets value. First come, first served. Once a class fills, that is it.

What is the difference between a Full Course Masterclass and an Exam Papers Class?

A Full Course Masterclass covers every concept and every technique for that paper, fully explained. An Exam Papers Class is a live walkthrough of past exam questions using those techniques. You need both to get the full result.

Can I book individual sessions instead of a bundle?

Yes. Most sessions are €20 each. Picking a Maths, Physics or Chemistry bundle saves you €10 and gives you every session in that subject.

I have a discount code. How does it work?

Click through to the LC HL or LC OL Maths Bundle, enter your code at checkout and €20 will come off automatically. Codes apply to the LC Maths bundles only.

Who is teaching what?

Cheze teaches all Maths, Applied Maths and the Junior Cycle Maths session. Ben Cullinane teaches HL Physics and HL Chemistry. Both are listed against every session in the timetable above.

Can I get a refund if I change my mind?

No refunds for change of mind. Seats are limited and reserving your place means a student who needed that seat could not get it. Once booked, your place is held. This is standard for crash-course programmes with fixed dates.

How do I ask a question before booking?

DM Cheze Grinds on Instagram or TikTok (@chezegrinds) or email chezegrinds@gmail.com. Fast replies.
