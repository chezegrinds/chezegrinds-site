FAQs

Common questions about the Elite Course

Who is this for?

The Elite 6-Week Revision Course is for 6th Year Leaving Cert Higher Level Maths students who want an intensive, structured push in the final weeks before the exam.

How many students are in the group?

Maximum 20. This is strictly limited to keep the group small enough for real interaction, personal feedback, and accountability.

What does the €97 deposit cover?

The €97 secures your place in the cohort. The remaining €300 is paid after, on a schedule that suits you. Or you can pay the full €397 upfront.

What if I miss a session?

Every session is recorded. Access 75+ hours of replays dating back to September. You can watch back at any time and never fall behind.

How is this different from the Academy?

The Academy is a 6-week revision programme with group classes and community support — open to all. The Elite Course is a more intensive 6-week programme with private sessions, smaller group (max 20), personal paper corrections, a weekly coaching session, and a private WhatsApp accountability group.
