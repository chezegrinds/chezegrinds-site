The Problem

Why students fall behind in HL Maths

These are the patterns we see again and again with students who come to us.

The pace is too fast

School classes move quickly. If a student misses one key concept, everything that builds on it falls apart. The gaps compound fast.

They do not know how to study maths

Most students re-read notes or watch videos passively. That does not build the problem-solving ability the exam demands.

Confidence drops before the exam

After a few bad results, students lose belief. They avoid maths, fall further behind, and start thinking they are just not a maths person.

Generic grinds do not target weak spots

A grind that covers random topics without knowing where the student actually struggles is a waste of time and money.

FAQ

Common questions

Which option is right for my child?

The Academy is ideal for students who want a structured 6-week programme with live group classes, on-demand resources, and community support — open to all. The Elite 6-Week Course is for students who want a more intensive, private-session experience with a smaller group, personal paper corrections, a weekly coaching session, and a private WhatsApp accountability group.

What year does my child need to be in?

Both the Academy and Elite 6-Week Course are designed for 5th and 6th Year Leaving Cert Higher Level students.

My child is really behind. Can they still catch up?

Yes. Many of our strongest results come from students who started well behind. The key is identifying the exact gaps and building a focused plan to close them. That is what we do from day one.

What does the €97 deposit cover?

The €97 deposit secures your child's place in the programme. The remaining balance is paid after you have started. The payment plan is flexible to suit you — pay in full or spread it out.

Is the Academy live or recorded?

Both. There are 3 hours of live classes every week on Zoom, and every session is recorded with full replay access. Students also get 24/7 access to the complete on-demand video library.

How quickly will we see results?

Most parents and students report a noticeable shift in confidence within the first few weeks. Grade improvements typically follow by the next school test or mock exam. The structured approach builds momentum fast.
